My Hometown

A brief description of my hometown.

Gresik, East Java, Indonesia.


Gresik is a regency in East Java province. It is bordered by Surabaya and the Madura Strait to the east, Lamongan to the west, the Java Sea to the north, Sidoarjo and Mojokerto to the south. Gresik also includes Bawean Island, which is 150 km off the Java Sea.

One of the traditional foods of Gresik City that is very well known. Nasi Krawu is one of the most popular culinary delights among the Gresik people.

Otak-otak Bandeng

Pudak


Pudak is one of the traditional foods of Gresik City that is no less delicious. Pudak is made from a mixture of coconut milk, brown sugar and rice flour.

Jubung

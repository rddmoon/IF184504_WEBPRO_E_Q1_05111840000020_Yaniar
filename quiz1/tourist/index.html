Tourist Attractions in Gresik


This is the list of my top 4 tourist attractions in my hometown.



1. Pulau Gili Noko


Gili Noko Island is popular for its exotic underwater scenery. Therefore, when traveling to Gili Niko you should try snorkeling. For the trip to Gili Niko, you can ride an engine boat from Bawean Island for about 20 minutes.

Apart from the enchanting underwater scenery, colorful small fish, coral rocks, and various other exotic marine biota, this island also offers beautiful scenery of white sand beaches with calm waves that are no less exotic.

2. Pantai Delegan


Beach is a tourist destination that must be visited while on vacation, for example, Delegan Beach Gresik. This beach attracts tourist with its natural scenery, white sand, and quite calm waves.

There are many activities that can be done at delegan beach, such as walking along the beach, swimming, or taking photos at various epic spots. There are plenty of food stalls and fun water play activities for children. Delegan Beach is located in Delegan Village, Panceng District.

3. Pulau Bawean


Bawean Island is a popular tourist destination for its breeding of deers. Not only that, there are also many amazing natural destinations such as the Pulau Cina Beach, Mombhul Beach, and beautiful waterfall tours.

In order to visit Bawean Island, you can take a fast boat from Gresik, but the price is quite expensive. Or you can get on a pioneer plane on Tuesday and Thursday from Surabaya. Enjoying the charm of Bawean Island is the right choice when you feel bored with the bustling urban atmosphere.

4. Banyu Urip Mangrove Center


One of the eco-tourism destinations in Gresik is Banyu Urip Mangrove Center. This lush mangrove forest was planted 17 years ago and presents a cool natural feel. There is a jogging track area along 600 meters and provides the natural beauty around it.

Not only walking around but you can also hunt for cool photo spots. You can also plant mangrove tree seeds here. The place is in Banyu Urip Village, Ujung Pangkah District.

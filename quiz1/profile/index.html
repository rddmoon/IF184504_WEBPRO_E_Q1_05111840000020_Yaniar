Hello :)

I'm Yaniar Pradityas Effendi. I'm a 20-year-old college student. I'm in my third year of studies towards a bachelor's in Informatics at Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS), Surabaya, Indonesia.

I was born in Gresik, East Java, Indonesia, and have been living there up until now. My MBTI personality type result is INTJ-A, not too accurate to describe me, though. Some people described me as a kind of quiet and reserved person, but my best friends stated otherwise. Some others described me as a diligent and serious type of a student. I can't help but laugh at that statement. I'm like, the most laid-back a person can be haha. My friends described me as a werd/unique/one of a kind/unsual person, because in most situations I would react differently.

I won't think too much about many things, it would just be a simple yes or no, or whether I like it or not. Mostly, I depend on my intuitions and be as realistic as I could. If you ask, does it work living like that?, you see, after about 20 years of my life, it works great for me. I won't describe the smallest details about myself, I will just write anything that I like. My life's pretty normal, though. I live my life in my own unique way. I'm really enjoying my life and just go with the flow. I guess you could say that I'm really grateful.

I love to take random photos, read, eat chocolates, and draw in my free time. There's this book series that I like and have been following since I was in elementary school, titled "Ulysses Moore" by the author Pierdomenico Baccalario, it's my favourite, but it's still ongoing. I also like reading some classics such as "Crime and Punishment", "Demian", "The Scarlet Letter", and etc. You see, it's mostly fiction books, but I also fancy some intriguing astronomy books, Carl Sagan's "Cosmos" and "Contact" are my favourites. It makes me reflect upon my life and understand how the universe works.

Yup. That's all about me. May you have a great day!
